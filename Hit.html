Hit

The most important thing is the setting of the supporting leg. It must be in close
proximity to the ball. The toe of the supporting leg should point in the direction you
want to punch. When hitting with a lift, you need to pull the toe - it must be
necessarily tense.

And also the flight of the ball depends on where you kick the ball.
